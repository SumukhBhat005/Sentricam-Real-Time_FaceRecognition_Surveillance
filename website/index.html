About the project

Intelligent surveillance, reimagined from the ground up.

SentriCam is a biometric surveillance platform that identifies and tracks individuals in real-time using state-of-the-art face recognition. Built for security monitoring, access control, and visitor management — all running locally on your machine.

No cloud dependency. No subscriptions. Your biometric data never leaves your device. 128-dimensional face embeddings are computed locally and matched instantly against your enrolled database.

Key Features

From enrollment to alert, every step is engineered.

01

Multi-Angle Enrollment

Capture 5 photos from different angles — front, left, right, up, and expression — for robust recognition accuracy.

02

30fps MJPEG Streaming

Hardware-accelerated camera capture with optimized JPEG encoding delivers smooth live video directly in your browser.

03

Jitter-Free Tracking

Dual-cascade detection with EMA smoothing ensures rock-solid bounding boxes without typical detector jitter.

04

Activity Logging

Every detection is timestamped and logged. Browse history, track unknown intrusions, and review system uptime.

05

Instant Alerts

Immediate visual alerts when an unrecognized individual is detected. Real-time status on the live camera HUD.

06

Configurable Thresholds

Fine-tune detection sensitivity, stream quality, and alert cooldowns in real-time — no restart required.
